About

Opened in 2000, Jurong East Sports Centre is the first one-stop integrated sports complex in Singapore, having facilities such as air-conditioned sport hall, stadium, fitness gym and swimming pools. Jurong East Sports Centre prides itself as a preferred venue for community events, tournaments and a leisure day out for all.
